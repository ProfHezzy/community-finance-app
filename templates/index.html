Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CoopSave

CoopSave combines the trust and community aspects of traditional Ajo/Esusu with modern banking security and convenience. Unlike banks that focus on individual accounts, we facilitate group savings with transparent tracking, automatic payouts, and social accountability features tailored for cooperative savings.

Our system sends automatic reminders as contribution deadlines approach. For chronic non-payment, group administrators can remove members (with majority vote in democratic groups). We also offer optional contribution insurance for groups that want guaranteed payouts regardless of individual defaults.

Yes! CoopSave supports members in 12 countries across Africa, Europe and North America. You can participate in groups across borders, with automatic currency conversion. Our system handles all the complexities of international transfers so your group can focus on saving.

Groups can choose between several payout methods: Random selection (traditional Ajo style), Fixed order (set at group creation), or Needs-based voting (members vote on who receives each payout). Our algorithm ensures complete fairness and transparency in all methods.

Absolutely. All funds are held in escrow accounts with our partner banks (GTB, Zenith, and Access Bank). We use 256-bit encryption, two-factor authentication, and regular third-party audits. Your money is always yours - we never lend or invest group funds.
